Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 July 2026

Overview

Mondrian Calendar is a visual calendar app that displays your Google Calendar events in a Mondrian-inspired layout.

Google Calendar data we access

With your permission, Mondrian Calendar uses the Google Calendar API with read-only scopes to access Google user data needed to display your calendar. This may include event titles, event start and end times, event descriptions, organisers, creators, and calendar display settings such as week start preferences.

Mondrian Calendar cannot create, edit, move, delete, invite guests to, or otherwise manage your Google Calendar events.

How we use your data

We use Google user data only to provide the app's calendar visualisation features. Specifically, we use your Google Calendar event data to render your schedule as weekly and monthly Mondrian-style calendar views, place events at the correct dates and times, show event details when you click an event, and apply your Calendar settings such as week start and hidden weekends.

Data storage

Mondrian Calendar processes Google Calendar data in your browser. The app does not operate its own server-side database and does not store your Google Calendar data on our servers. Access tokens are held in browser memory for the current app session and are not intentionally written to long-term storage by the app.

Data retention

Because the app does not store your Google Calendar data on our servers, we do not retain copies of your calendar data after your browser session ends or after you sign out of the app.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, transfer, or share your Google user data with third parties. Your Google Calendar data is requested from Google and displayed in your browser for your use of Mondrian Calendar.

Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect user data, including secure HTTPS connections.

Revoking access

You can revoke Mondrian Calendar’s access to your Google Account at any time from your Google Account permissions page.
