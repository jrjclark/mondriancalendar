Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 July 2026

Overview

Mondrian Calendar is a visual calendar app that displays your Google Calendar events in a Mondrian-inspired layout.

Google Calendar data we access

With your permission, Mondrian Calendar may access read-only Google Calendar information, such as calendar names, event titles, event times, and related event metadata needed to display your schedule.

How we use your data

We use your Google Calendar data only to display your calendar inside the app. We do not create, edit, or delete calendar events.

Data storage

Mondrian Calendar processes calendar data in your browser and does not store your calendar data on our servers.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your Google Calendar data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect user data, including secure HTTPS connections.

Revoking access

You can revoke Mondrian Calendar’s access to your Google Account at any time from your Google Account permissions page.
